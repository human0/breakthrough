Counseling Services for Families of Drug and Alcohol Addicts

Addiction Affects Patients’ Families

Addiction can impact families just as powerfully as it does patients. The stress of dealing with a family member’s addiction impacts the stability of the home, finances, and the family’s unity. When a loved one is suffering from an addiction, family members may be unwilling to admit to the problem, or be unsure of the best way to support someone on their journey to sobriety.

How to Support Your Loved One

Family members need to recognize and accept that their loved one’s addiction is a chronic disease. It’s not a personal or moral failing, but rather a disorder that has physically taken over the decision and reward center of the brain. Addiction’s effects can be reversed over time with the help of addiction treatment centers such as Breakthrough Addiction Treatment Center.

Family Recovery

Breakthrough Addiction Recovery Center recognizes the importance of helping the families of those with chemical dependencies to work toward their own recovery. Mental and emotional rehabilitation of patient’s families and loved ones is crucial to their own health as well as the sustained abstinence of the former addict. We offer counseling services for families and continued support and guidance through the recovery process.

Opiate Addiction Rehab Maintenance & Support – Staying Sober for Good

Breakthrough Addiction Recovery Centre unique program of medication assisted treatment and counseling in the patient’s natural environment opens the door for patients to achieve long-term sobriety. We believe in sustainable addiction treatment. We believe treatment should change and grow with a person as they experience recovery. Because of this, Breakthrough Addiction Recovery Center offers cost effective, long-term maintenance treatment solutions.

Because patients aren’t required to leave their day-to-day lives, they learn to effectively cope with stress and triggers that may make them want to abuse opiates/ alcohol again.

Breakthrough Addiction Recovery Center program is also appropriate for patients who have completed treatment in a residential center, but still want on going after care support on their journey to sobriety.

Medication Assisted Treatment

Vivitrol, Revia ( Naltrexone & Assisted Treatment for Opiate & Alcohol Addiction ) How Medication Assisted Treatment Works

Medication assisted treatment can help treat the physical side of addiction by reducing cravings and easing withdrawal symptoms. The medications used in addiction treatment target the region of the brain that creates the overwhelming urges to use opioids or alcohol, even though opioid /alcohol use is self-destructive.

Medications for Opiate Addiction Treatment

Breakthrough Addiction Recovery Centre uses a variety of FDA approved medication to reduce cravings and to protect patients from withdrawal symptoms as well as relapse prevention. Medications used include Vivitrol (Naltrexone injectable), Revia (non-addictive non-narcotic blocking agent, Naltrexone), as an anti-craving medication and for relapse prevention. Naltrexone is an opioid antagonist that binds to opioid receptors but does not trigger the brain’s pleasure response. If a patient does relapse and use opioid/alcohol while being treated with naltrexone, he or she will not be able to get chemically high. The patient won’t feel the euphoric effects of the drugs/alcohol, and will have wasted time and money on opiates/alcohol.
